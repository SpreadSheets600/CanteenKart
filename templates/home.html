Our Story

About CanteenKart

CanteenKart was created by students, for students. We understand the challenges of campus life - limited time between classes, budget constraints, and the need for nutritious food to fuel your studies.

Our mission is to revolutionize the campus dining experience by offering quality food at affordable prices with the convenience of online ordering and quick pickup.
